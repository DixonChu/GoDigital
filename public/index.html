My SmartPortal

MySmart Portal is an internal project management tool to help you manage your construction projects better.

Features


Site Records

Now everyone in your team can contribute to the same daily site diary, making it as accurate as possible.

Files Sharing

Upload your files in one place so that your team members can download them anywhere at anytime.

RFIs Management

Keep track and send RFIs (available to attach files) to one portal where consultants can answer them seamlessly. Email notifications available.

Tasks Allocation

Making sure every tasks will not be forgotten after allocating to the person in charged. Email notifications available.

Photos Gallery

Upload all your photos at one place so that your team can find them easily.

Make Orders

Send purchase orders via email to your suppliers directly from MySmart Portal. They will be recorded automatically in a list view.
